About Me

Have you ever thought to yourself there is more to life than what you are currently seeing, doing, or living? Me too; even from the earliest of my days, I can remember having this thought.

I am a mother, a wife, an Army Veteran and have worked with entrepreneurs for the past 15 years. My relationship with NLP started with my eldest child. I watched my adult daughter go through Neuro-Linguistics Programming and witnessed firsthand the tremendous impact mindset development had on her. She went from being a young lady with low self-esteem, little self-love, and trust issues to blossoming into a young woman who found purpose and gratitude for life. It was a HUGE transformation. When she asked if I would ever consider taking NLP training, I immediately thought, heck yes! If NLP training could have such a profound impact on my daughter, what would it have in store for me?

Remember earlier when I said I had always thought there was more to life than what I was currently seeing, doing, or living? Well, there is, and NLP is that more for me! I began to see myself in a new light with new possibilities. I was imprinted from an early age, which we all are, to no fault of the significant adults in our lives. They did the best they could with the tools they had. My toolbox had just been upgraded, and wowzers, what a profound impact it had on me. I discovered I struggled with self-worth issues, limiting beliefs about money and abundance, and lack of self-love, all handed down to me by my parents, unbeknownst to them.

What I learned was we are all imprinted in our early years by the significant adults in our lives and that is where we developed our values and beliefs, which may not be in alignment with success. Having this knowledge compelled me to invent a better version of myself, which let’s face it, I’m still a work in progress, AND am much different today than I was pre-NLP. I now live in a world where anything is possible. AND if anything is possible, can we agree everything is possible? I want to share my knowledge of NLP with other business owners who dream of, desire, and deserve success!

My military training gave me the tools to be disciplined and mission focused. NLP has given me the tools to know that I have all the resources within me to excel at anything I put my mind and heart into. My experience from working with entrepreneurs over the past 15 years has shown me the many struggles business owners face when building a successful business. My passion is to take all of these components of my life and help other business owners achieve their dreams and desires they deserve.
